About Us Page

Welcome to Crypto Wallet web app.

We are just simple wanna-be JAVA developers. Our application helps you keep trace of your crypto finances.

Inside our virtual wallet, you can register any crypto coin you buy from other trading platforms.

The wallet gives you the possibility to see different statistics about your registered coins (such as USD price, daily market fluctuation etc).
